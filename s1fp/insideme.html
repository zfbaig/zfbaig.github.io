Inside me,
2022, oil on canvas, 18 x 24"
The dance of Lord Shiva represents life as a cycle of creation, preservation, and destruction. My performance of that dance entails felt experiences of my grandmother’s garden, cricket games with my grandfather, crumbling minarets of the Old City, and the pink curtains of my childhood bedroom that are an internal reality that will always be inside me. The multi-faceted past is not only a part of us but it provides a foundation for the future that lies ahead. I was inspired by Frida Kahlo’s use of symbols and surrealist juxtapositions in her self-portraits to represent moments in her life, her emotions, and ultimately her identity.
